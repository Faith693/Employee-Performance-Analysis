# 📊 Employee Performance Analysis

## 🧩 Business Problem
Organizations need to understand employee performance drivers to improve productivity and workforce effectiveness. This project analyzes performance, training, and satisfaction metrics to identify key trends and improvement areas.

---

## 📁 Dataset
The dataset includes:
- Employee performance ratings
- Training hours
- Job satisfaction scores
- Department and job role
- Years at company
- Recruitment source

---

## 🛠 Tools Used
- Power BI (Dashboard and visualization)
- Excel / SQL (Data preparation and analysis)

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Average performance across the organization is moderate (~3.0), indicating room for improvement
- Employees with more training hours tend to have higher performance levels
- Certain departments consistently outperform others
- Performance varies across recruitment sources and branches
- Job satisfaction is closely linked to employee performance

---

## 💡 Recommendations
- Increase investment in employee training programs
- Focus on underperforming departments with targeted interventions
- Optimize recruitment channels that yield higher-performing employees
- Improve employee engagement to boost satisfaction and performance

---

## 📌 Conclusion
This analysis provides actionable insights to help organizations enhance employee performance and overall workforce productivity.
